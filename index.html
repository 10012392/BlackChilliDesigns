Welcome to Black Chilli Designs, thanks for dropping by. Here at Black Chilli Designs we are able to design and manufacture products for an almost limitless variety of applications including branding, advertising, retail displays, events, expo's, hospitality, domestic and commercial just to name a few.

Taking the concept or idea and turning it into an amazing, cool, functional reality is the name of the game. Working with creative people is always exciting and Black Chilli Designs can offer design input, original thinking and the pratical experience required to make it happen.

Here on the Black Chilli Design website you will find a few products we have built for previous projects we have worked on. You can use the images as inspiration, blend them with your imagination and let Black Chilli Designs help you turn your ideas into unique creations.
