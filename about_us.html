About Black Chilli Designs

Black Chilli Designs started as just a design and manufacturing company but we also started to hire items for different events and promotions as we were finding that some people wanted to hire certain items for a fun event and there is a market there that doesn't bring imagination to life.

We love to work with creative people and design different and new products and show everyone what we can do.
