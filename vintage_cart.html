Concession Cart

A vintage style cart for any display, promotion, and concession purposes. This one is finished in white but can be supplied in almost any colour. Vintage carts fit any number of retail displays such as tableware, wine, flowers, glassware etc. This can be used in restaurants as a desert, candy and drink bar or for promoting company brands.

Designed for order as they are completely customisable with colours, logo's, branding, size and storage requirements. Carts can also be specified with canopy's and even freezers for ice-cream or gelato.

Size shown - 1700mm width x 650mm dimention x 900mm height
