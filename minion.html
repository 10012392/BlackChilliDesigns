Minion

We wanted a project to show whats possible to create using a combination of different materials, timber, mdf, flexiply, polystyrene, plastic pipe, foam coat, vinyl graphics, paint and fabric. Standing 1300mm high a minion lent itself perfectly to this idea and everyone loves minions.

The concept isn't to sell minions, which are copyrighted, but to demonstrate the type of products we can create for retail displays, expo's, marketing, branding, advertising and events.
